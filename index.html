About Me

Born in Chitwan and educated at Durbar High School, I learned the value of hard work early in life. At 20, I traveled to India and successfully ran a restaurant in Goa for over 18 years, gaining strong leadership and management experience.

Returning to Nepal, I dedicated myself to social service. Today, as Chairman of RSP-Khairhani, I stand committed to honest leadership and community development.

The Beginning

Education & Early Career

Completed education at Durbar High School. Studied Hotel Management at Discovery Nepal Pvt Ltd. Started a restaurant in Goa, India, running it successfully for over 15 years.

Return to Nepal

Social Service

Returned to Nepal to serve the community. Served as Chitwan District President of 'Contribution for Nation'. Became a key figure in local politics.

Present Day

RSP Khairahani

Joined RSP–Khairahani at its early stage. Played a pivotal role in growing the organization to over 2,000 members in Khairahani.
