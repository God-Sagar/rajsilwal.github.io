About Me

Born in Chitwan and educated at Durbar High School, I learned the value of hard work early in life. At 20, I traveled to India and successfully ran a restaurant in Goa for over 18 years, gaining strong leadership and management experience. Returning to Nepal, I dedicated myself to social service. Today, as Chairman of RSP-Khairhani, I stand committed to honest leadership and a better future.

The Beginning

Education & Early Career

Completed education at Durbar High School. Studied Hotel Management at Discovery Nepal Pvt Ltd. Started a restaurant in Goa, India, running it successfully for over 15 years.
