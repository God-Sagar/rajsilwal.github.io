About Me

Born in Chitwan and educated at Darbar High School, I learned the value of hard work early in life. At 20, I went to India and went on to successfully run a restaurant in Goa for over 18 years, gaining strong leadership and management experience. Returning to Nepal, I dedicated myself to social service. Today, as Chairman of RSP-Khairhani, I stand committed to honest leadership and a better future.
